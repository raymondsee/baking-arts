Classes

Baking Arts classes are designed to accommodate the novice chef as well as the avid home cook looking to master new techniques.

We offer a relaxed, friendly teaching style that encompasses theory, demonstration and full hands-on participation. Classes are designed so that each student completes their own recipes and projects from start to finish to ensure a true learning experience.

In addition to our calendar classes, Baking Arts is available for private parties and classes from 1 to 10 people. We also offer gift certificates that make a terrific gift for that special someone.

How to Register

To register for a class you can enroll on line through our class calendar or call 415 706-7112.

Calendar Classes

Calendar classes are scheduled throughout the year and are limited to a maximum of eight students. Please see our calendar for a list of scheduled group classes. The calendar is routinely updated, so please check back for newly scheduled classes.

Private Classes

Private classes may be scheduled any day of the week. The classes are conducted one on one with the pastry chef, or you may book the class with one or more other students. Class pricing is based on the number of students below. Saturday and Sunday private class bookings require a minumum of 5 people. All food, materials and handouts are included in the cost of the class. Some select classes may have an additional fee based on materials and length of class.

Cancellations and Refunds

Refunds will be issued for any class cancelled with 10 (ten) or more days notice prior to class date.

No refunds or credits will be given for classes cancelled with less than 10 (ten) days notice or for no shows. If you are unable to make a class, consider letting a friend attend in your absence.

We appreciate your understanding there are no exceptions to our cancellation and refund policy at any time or for any reason. Please do not call us to ask for an exception to our policy.

Baking Arts may cancel any class due to low enrollment. You will be contacted and a full refund made.

We reserve the right to substitute ingredients based on seasonality and available quality.
